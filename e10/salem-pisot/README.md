# Weyl Spectrum

This repository contains computational and expository resources on the Weyl spectrum associated with the Coxeter group \(W(E_{10})\), including recursive constructions of Salem and Pisot numbers and related data tables.

## Data files

- `primitive-salem-first-1688.csv`: complete list of the first 1688 primitive Salem numbers currently included in the table.
- `primitive-salem-level-1.csv`: newly emerging primitive Salem numbers at \(s_0\)-level 1.
- `primitive-salem-level-2.csv`: newly emerging primitive Salem numbers at \(s_0\)-level 2.
- `primitive-salem-level-3.csv`: newly emerging primitive Salem numbers at \(s_0\)-level 3.
- `primitive-salem-level-4.csv`: newly emerging primitive Salem numbers at \(s_0\)-level 4.
- `primitive-salem-level-5.csv`: newly emerging primitive Salem numbers at \(s_0\)-level 5.

The column `s0_level` refers to the level with respect to the generator \(s_0\).
